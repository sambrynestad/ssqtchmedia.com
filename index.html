About our team

Every company has a creation story, and SSQTCH Media’s is like no other. Four science majors began filming and editing sophomore year of college. With no prior knowledge in film and media, these four saw things from a unique perspective. SSQTCH’s foundation has been about viewing everything differently. Redefining film and editing makes SSQTCH incomparable with the rest, pushing the limits through cinematography and editing through the eyes of science. These four gentlemen have the passion to share yours through a different eye. SSQTCH is always open to any sort of video concept, from music videos to about us to documentary/interview videos. SSQTCH is always open to increasing their knowledge in every aspect of video.
